Admissions 2025–26

Your Journey
Begins Here

We welcome students who are curious, driven, and ready to make their mark. Our admissions process is designed to understand the whole child — not just grades.

1
Submit Application
Complete the online form with academic records, personal statement, and parent details. Applications open year-round.
2
Campus Interview
An informal meeting with our admissions team. We want to get to know your child's passions, not just their grades.
3
Assessment Day
A short assessment to understand learning style and academic placement — designed to be low-pressure and enjoyable.
4
Enrollment & Welcome
Receive your offer, complete enrollment, and join our orientation week to meet teachers, peers, and campus.
